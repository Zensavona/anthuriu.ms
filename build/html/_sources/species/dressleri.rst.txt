=====================
Anthurium *Dressleri*
=====================

:Section: Cardiolonchium


Defining Characteristics
------------------------

A. *Dressleri* has a similar leaf shape to Crystallinum, with less notable venation. The backs of the leaves display red veining, the emerging leaves are a dark reddish tone and the petiole is ribbed.
