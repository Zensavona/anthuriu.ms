Anthurium Dressleri¶

Section

Cardiolonchium

Defining Characteristics¶

  1. Dressleri has a similar leaf shape to Crystallinum, with less notable venation. The backs of the leaves display red veining, the emerging leaves are a dark reddish tone and the petiole is ribbed.

Variations¶

There is a “dark” varaint cultivated by Jay Vannini
